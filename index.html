About

Rooted in community, designed for momentum.

ToC exists to make sure African Americans are among the technology influencers, builders, and decision-makers shaping the future. The refreshed site should make that mission visible, measurable, and easy to join.

ConnectBring professionals, founders, students, and career changers together.
